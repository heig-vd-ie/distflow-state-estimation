Introduction

In pandapower documentation , under Signing System, the sign depend if it’s in consumer point of view or generator In this case, the edge is a branch, it can be considered as a consumer. Produced energy is negative and consumed energy is positive.

And the vector always enter in an equipment, the sign is determine as specified below.

The following image is a schema of a PI DistFlow branch in a distribution network.

In Distflow Schema, the edge term is used to represent a branch, switch and transformer.

The equation (1) is the active and reactive power inside the equipment from upstream node and (2) from downstream node.

\begin{align} \underline{S_{flow}^{i}} \end{align}
\begin{align} \overline{S_{flow}^{j}} \end{align}
\begin{align} \overline{S_{flow}^{k}} \end{align}
\begin{align} \overline{S_{flow}^{m}} \end{align}

The term \(B\) is the transversal element of the branch, it is positive for capacitance and negative for inductance.

The parameter is defined as follows:

Term

Comment
\[\underline{S_{flow}^{j}}\]

Active and reactive power inside the equipment from upstream node
\[\overline{S_{flow}^{j}}\]

Active and reactive power inside the equipment from downstream node
